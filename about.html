About Us

Maryvic International School

Maryvic International school, Port Harcourt Nigeria where emerging leaders are raised. we are proud of the tradition of excellence and innovation we are building. We are particularly blessed with certified, loving, practice-proven, hard-working, resourceful professionals. Partnerships and co-operation between parents, students, and staffs have made our school community vibrant and inviting.

What we do

MaryVic International School is a private school committed to training children mentally, physically, emotionally, socially and spiritually. We are fully trained and equipped for Montessori training of children. We run a blend of Nigeria and British Curriculum towards a solid early formation of positive behavioural change in our children.

How we do it

We provide our pupils with conducive learning environment, furnished with state-of-the-art facilities, and managed by well trained and dedicated professionals. Our teachers are fully trained and qualified to teach children in the various fields of learning. Our unique teaching methodologies turn learning into fun for our pupils and students.

Where we do it

We have our academic complex, housing the Nursery, Basic and Secondary arms of Education at No. 247 Ada George Road, close to Romans Filling Station, Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. MaryVic International School have all the class levels needed to train your child from birth through Secondary education and our quality speaks for us.

Welcome to Maryvic International School, a beacon of educational excellence.

Founded by the late Lady Rosa Wenah (Ma. P), our mission is to provide exceptional educational services that foster well-rounded individuals capable of making significant contributions to Nigerian society and the global community.

At Maryvic International School, we cater to students from Pre-School through to Primary and Secondary education, ensuring a comprehensive learning journey that nurtures academic prowess and personal growth. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to optimize learning outcomes across various domains, providing learners with unparalleled educational opportunities.


Our management team, led by Mrs. Elizabeth Bobo Sofiri-Brown as Administrator, comprises seasoned educationists and administrators dedicated to the success and holistic development of every student. They are supported by a team of highly skilled professionals who are committed to delivering quality education.
Join us at Maryvic International School, where academic excellence meets nurturing care. We eagerly await your enrollment as we prepare students for a future of success and fulfillment.

Our Vision and Mission

  • Raising a community of purpose-driven children through functional and adaptive learning for God and humanity.

  • At Maryvic International school, pupil's learning is our priority.

  • We believe that every child can learn to their fullest potential, developing individual talent and technology skills by being actively engaged in the learning process.

  • Here, we raise children who are well-rounded, God fearing, disciplined and self-directed.

  • We achieve this by providing equitable and rigorous instructions that will close gaps and accelerate the learning of every child.


Why Maryvic?

If you could, you would probably spend every moment with your child, but as a working parent, you have to earn a living. Maryvic recognises the importance of balance to parents: the need to successfully juggle work and family life and to find a school that cater for the educational, emotional and physical needs of your child….that gives you peace of mind! Cotville provides just that!

Once you enroll your child at Maryvic, we become partners. Our goal is to ensure that you feel relaxed at work or wherever else you need to be, knowing that your child is cared for in a safe, loving and simulating environment that is healthy for his or her growth and development. The children are given the individual attention, resources and space in which to grow intellectually, physically and emotionally.

I am proud of our comprehensive co-curricular programme that develops our children’s confidence and esteem while our Curriculum is under constant review to reflect best global practice. The enthusiasm and experience of our outstanding staff faculty, is the driving force behind all our activities. That is why in addition to big hearts, we make sure that our staff possess adequate training in early childhood development, and are consistently been trained in best educational practices both locally and internationally.

We also invest in and constantly update our state -of -the art educational and recreational facilities. And, of course, we involve you every step of the way, encouraging discussion on the welfare and progress of your children through various forums that are organised by the school.
