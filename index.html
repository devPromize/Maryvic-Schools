Welcome to Maryvic

Maryvic International School is a place to learn and grow together via our hands-on curriculum. With a global approach to teaching and nuturing in an ideal learning environment. Maryvic International School is the school of choice reputed for excellence in academics, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. Our education style is designed to create an interactive atmosphere between the staff, students and parents through both academic and non-academic activities. This system allows our pupils develop socially, morally and spiritually. Maryvic International School is one that ensures every child is nurtured and groomed to achieve their highest potential. At Maryvic, your children are the focus of our passion. In everything we do, our efforts go toward ensuring the children portray excellence in their academic and moral pursuits. Our joy is rooted in seeing our pupils grow and progress successfully through the many phases of life. Here, moulding your children and empowering them to make excellent global impact is our ultimate priority. Once again, welcome to Maryvic School.

What we do

MaryVic International School is a private school committed to training children mentally, physically, emotionally, socially and spiritually. We are fully trained and equipped for Montessori training of children. We run a blend of Nigeria and British Curriculum towards a solid early formation of positive behavioural change in our children.

How we do it

We provide our pupils with conducive learning environment, furnished with state-of-the-art facilities, and managed by well trained and dedicated professionals. Our teachers are fully trained and qualified to teach children in the various fields of learning. Our unique teaching methodologies turn learning into fun for our pupils and students.

Where we do it

We have our academic complex, housing the Nursery, and Basic arms of Education at No. 247 Ada George Road, close to Romans Filling Station, Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. MaryVic International School have all the class levels needed to train your child from birth through Secondary education and our quality speaks for us.
